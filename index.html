// 06 — Uniqueness Analysis

Estimated Fingerprint Uniqueness
—
Research finding: Studies by the EFF (Panopticlick) and others show that browser fingerprinting alone can uniquely identify approximately 83–94% of users without any cookies. Combining IP + fingerprint raises this above 99%.

How to protect yourself: Use Firefox with the arkenfox user.js config, Tor Browser (which normalizes fingerprints across users), or Brave (which adds noise to canvas/audio APIs). Browser extensions like Canvas Blocker add randomization. A VPN helps mask IP but does not stop fingerprinting.
